# SPERB Iteration Protocol

This document governs how SPERB evolves beyond v1.0. The framework is designed to improve over time through structured open consultation.

---

## Versioning Convention

- **PATCH** (v1.0.1): Clarifications, documentation fixes, tooling bugs — no scoring impact
- **MINOR** (v1.1): Rubric clarifications with minimal scoring impact, new tooling, cultural calibration notes
- **MAJOR** (v2.0): New dimensions, weighting changes, tier boundary adjustments, rubric overhauls

---

## The v2.0 Development Process

### Stage 1 — Open Commentary (6 months before release)
A public consultation issue is opened on GitHub. Any party may submit proposals via the [Dimension Proposal template](../.github/ISSUE_TEMPLATE/dimension_proposal.md). Proposals are categorised:
- Clarification
- Rubric refinement
- New dimension
- Weighting adjustment
- Tier boundary change

### Stage 2 — Advisory Panel Review
An advisory panel of at least 7 members reviews submissions. Composition must include:
- ≥1 regulatory representative
- ≥2 academic researchers (AI ethics or media studies)
- ≥2 industry practitioners (brand, agency, or platform)
- ≥1 consumer or audience advocate
- ≥1 synthetic persona operator

Panel deliberations are published in full.

### Stage 3 — Draft and Validation
A draft v2.0 specification is published. A validation pilot re-scores the original 10-entity cohort under the proposed framework. Results published for community review.

### Stage 4 — Formal Release
v2.0 published with changelog and migration guide explaining how v1.0 scores translate.

---

## Anticipated v2.0 Proposals

These items are pre-logged from the v1.0 design process for v2.0 consideration:

1. **Differential weighting** — AIDS, ECS, and/or SCES may carry more than 10 points
2. **Environmental dimension** — carbon cost per AI-generated content unit
3. **Updated PVS rubric** — anchored to 2026+ generation tool capabilities
4. **Cultural relativism calibration** — AIDS disclosure norms vary across markets
5. **Video corpus scoring** — currently under-specified for video-heavy entities
6. **Longitudinal scoring protocol** — same entity at multiple time points
7. **Third-party audit pathway** — accredited evaluator certification process

---

## Submitting a Proposal

Open an issue using the [Dimension Proposal template](../.github/ISSUE_TEMPLATE/dimension_proposal.md). Proposals should include:
- The specific change proposed
- The rationale (evidence-anchored)
- The expected scoring impact if applied to the pilot cohort
- Any trade-offs or unintended consequences

Proposals without evidence or rationale will be closed.
